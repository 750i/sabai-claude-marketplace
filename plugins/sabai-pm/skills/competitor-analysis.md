## Research Sources

- **Product:** Sign up, demos, free trials
- **Pricing:** Website, sales calls
- **Reviews:** G2, Capterra, TrustRadius
- **Tech:** BuiltWith, StackShare, job postings
- **News:** Crunchbase, press releases, blogs
- **Social:** LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube

## Update Frequency

- **Tier 1 competitors:** Monthly
- **Tier 2 competitors:** Quarterly
- **Emerging threats:** As discovered
- **After major announcements:** Within 1 week
